Anna (Beck) Lawler | Cartographer

I am a cartographer and human geographer who uses maps and data visualizations to understand complex social and environmental relationships. I view mapmaking as a methodological and analytic tool that visualizes spatial patterns that affect people and the planet. Maps are also powerful tools for communicating complex phenomena while telling stories that can create positive change.
