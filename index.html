Anna Beck | Cartographer

I am a human geographer who embraces cartography and data visualization as an avenue into understanding complex social and environmental relationships. I believe that visualization has the power to help students and the general public better understand these complex phenomena while telling stories that enable more just outcomes. For me visualizing these complex stories is a political process through which I actively seek to counter popular narratives that are destructive and collaboratively tell the stories of people who struggle to be heard and represented fairly.
